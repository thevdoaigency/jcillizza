About

I'm James Cillizza, a mixed-media artist and designer exploring the chaos, contradiction, and absurdity embedded in everyday life. My work is a visual manifestation of organized chaos—where precision collides with spontaneity, where pop culture icons dissolve into abstraction, and where meaning dissolves into pure emotional expression.

Through abstract expressionism, geometric layering, pop art subversion, and textural experimentation, I investigate the gap between how we perceive order and the fundamental randomness beneath. My practice pulls from street art, fine art, design, and the visual noise of contemporary culture—nothing is sacred, everything is material for reexamination.

Each piece asks: What if we stopped trying to make sense of it all? What if the contradiction itself is the point? My work doesn't resolve these questions—it amplifies them, inviting viewers into the beautiful mess of being alive in an inherently absurd world.
